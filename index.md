AngelVoice Privacy Policy (Draft)

Effective date: 2026-02-01

Summary
This app is designed to help users communicate with image-based word cards. We do not collect or sell personal data. All data is stored locally on the device unless you explicitly export or share it.

Data We Store
- Profiles (names you create in the app)
- Word cards you add or edit
- App settings (grid rows/columns, display name)

Where Data Is Stored
- Data is stored locally on your device in app storage.
- The app does not upload data to any server.

Permissions
- Photos/Media access is used only to let you choose images for word cards.
- The app does not access your photos unless you explicitly choose an image.

Children's Privacy
This app is intended to help children and others who have speech difficulties. We do not collect personal information from children. If you believe we have stored data you wish to remove, please contact us.

Sharing
We do not share data with third parties.

Security
Data is stored on-device and protected by the device's security features. No network transmission occurs.
